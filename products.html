Our Products

At Bralison EPZ Ltd, we specialize in exporting a variety of premium fresh farm produce to meet the needs of our international clients. Our products are sourced from trusted farms that adhere to sustainable and ethical farming practices.

Fruits

Potatoes

Select varieties of potatoes grown in fertile Kenyan soils, prized for their uniform size, smooth skin, and versatility in cooking. Suitable for baking, boiling, frying, and processing into value-added products.

Onions

High-grade onions with excellent firmness and pungency, cultivated under optimal conditions to ensure long shelf life and outstanding taste. Perfect for fresh consumption, cooking, and industrial processing.

Chillies

Premium quality fresh chillies sourced from trusted Kenyan farms, known for their vibrant color, crisp texture, and characteristic spicy flavor. Ideal for adding heat and flavor to a wide range of culinary dishes.
