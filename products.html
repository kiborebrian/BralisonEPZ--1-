Our Products

At Bralison EPZ Ltd, we specialize in exporting a variety of premium fresh farm produce to meet the needs of our international clients. Our products are sourced from trusted farms that adhere to sustainable and ethical farming practices.

Fruits

AVOCADO (HASS, FUERTE, JUMBO)

Creamy, nutrient-rich avocados grown in Kenya's fertile highlands. Hass variety features pebbly skin and rich flavor, Fuerte offers smooth texture, while Jumbo provides exceptional size. Perfect for guacamole, salads, and healthy fats.

MANGOES

Juicy tropical mangoes bursting with sweet, aromatic flavor. Kenya's sun-ripened varieties include Kent, Tommy Atkins, and Apple mangoes, prized for their vibrant orange flesh, smooth texture, and natural sweetness.

PURPLE PASSION FRUIT

Intensely aromatic passion fruit with wrinkled purple skin and golden pulp. Bursting with tropical flavor, tangy sweetness, and crunchy seeds. Perfect for juices, desserts, and exotic flavor enhancement.

PINEAPPLES

Sweet, golden pineapples with tropical fragrance and juicy flesh. Kenya's ideal climate produces perfectly balanced sugar-acid ratio, making them exceptional for fresh eating, juices, and tropical desserts.

TAMARILLO

Exotic tree tomato with smooth, egg-shaped fruit and vibrant red or orange skin. Offers unique sweet-tart flavor reminiscent of tomatoes and passion fruit. Perfect for sauces, chutneys, and fresh consumption.

PAWPAW

Sweet, tropical papaya with vibrant orange flesh and black seeds. Rich in enzymes and vitamins, offering honey-like sweetness and buttery texture. Ideal for fresh eating, smoothies, and digestive health.

Herbs

TARRAGON

Aromatic herb with distinctive anise-like flavor, prized for its delicate leaves and essential oils. Perfect for enhancing sauces, dressings, and poultry dishes with its subtle licorice notes.

ROSEMARY

Robust evergreen herb with needle-like leaves and piney fragrance. Excellent for seasoning meats, vegetables, and breads, offering intense aromatic flavor and natural preservative properties.

LEMON GRASS

Citrus-scented grass with bright, lemony flavor and subtle ginger notes. Ideal for teas, soups, curries, and aromatherapy applications, known for its refreshing and digestive properties.

OREGANO

Classic Mediterranean herb with bold, peppery flavor and warm aromatic notes. Essential for Italian, Greek, and Mexican cuisines, perfect for pizzas, pasta sauces, and grilled vegetables.

THYME

Delicate herb with earthy, minty flavor and subtle floral notes. Versatile seasoning for soups, stews, roasted vegetables, and meats, offering gentle warmth and complexity to dishes.

CHIVES

Mild onion-flavored herb with slender green stalks and purple flowers. Perfect for garnishing, dips, salads, and delicate seasoning, adding fresh color and subtle allium flavor to dishes.

MINT

Refreshing herb with cool, crisp flavor and invigorating aroma. Ideal for beverages, desserts, salads, and Middle Eastern dishes, known for its digestive benefits and cooling properties.
