ABOUT US

We are a dedicated grower and exporter of safe and quality fresh fruits and vegetables from Kenya; products of an outstanding quality, with guaranteed food safety and grown under circumstances with attention to people and the environment. We achieve this by striving and consistently giving our products with discretion, passion and commitment.

Our values lie on the perpetual commitment to our customers, quality control, food safety & security, business practices and our employees.

The safety and quality in our products is a fundamental value, as well as the safety of our workers and the environment respect. We are committed to our policy of bringing you only the freshest, most nutritious, and most importantly, safest produce available.

When we say we offer ‘Every season, every day’, it is a promise; a promise we take very seriously. With Bralison, safety and freshness of our produce takes center-stage, as we work hard to bring your customers a comforting slice of home and a tantalizing taste of the exotic

OUR COMMITMENTS

Bralison’s leadership team is committed to:

CUSTOMERS:

We recognize our customers are essential to our success, and we are committed to continuously improve our processes to provide quality products and services that exceed their expectations.

QUALITY:

Quality is Bralison’s number one goal. We are committed to employing total quality in every aspect of our business - in our employees, our operations, and our processes, to provide top quality products and services to our customers.

FOOD SAFETY & SECURITY:

We are committed to supplying our customers and the marketplace with fruits and vegetables that are safe for consumption by our customers. We ensure total security from the field to the store.

BUSINESS PRACTICES:

We are committed to utilizing ethical business practices, state-of-the-art technology, and good management practices to sustain progressive growth and customer satisfaction.

Our Team

Our skilled team comprises agricultural experts, quality assurance specialists, logistics coordinators, and a dedicated management team. Together, we ensure timely delivery of fresh produce that meets international standards.
